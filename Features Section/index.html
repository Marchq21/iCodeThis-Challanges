Features

Boards

Projects are represented as boards in the system. Along with displaying essential information about the project, they can be set to public or private, depending on th user's preferences.

Tasks

Tasks and subtasks are the smallest chunks of executable assignments. Tasks can be assigned to multiple users, given a priority and easily moved across different states in the development pipeline.

Members

Users invited to work together with the common goal of delivering a successful project. They can have several different roles as active members of the development, or simply followers of the board.

Timer

THe time tracking feature enables the users to easily measure the time spent on a specific task. Once tracked and logged, the time can be seen and edited in a dedicated Time view for additional control.

Follow

Ahead offers the possibility to follow the development of a project and be notified of its activities. Users can follow entire boards, or they can choose to follow just specific tasks within a board.

Recently Accessed

The boards that you are actively working onjcan be easily accessed with the Recently Accessed feature.
